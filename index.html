About

A Fabric mod for MC 1.21–1.21.10 that rigs casino-style minigames. Open the in-game settings menu with P to configure each module. (Note: P conflicts with Social Interactions — unbind it in MC Controls first.)

Keybinds

POpen Donut Casino settings menu
OToggle HUD overlay on/off
GToggle Paper Game rig
HSwitch Paper Game active side (A ↔ B)
JToggle Blackjack rig
KToggle Blackjack Force 21
UToggle 50/50 rig
IToggle Russian Roulette rig
LRussian Roulette trigger (hold)

HUD Overlay

A small indicator displays in the top-left corner of the screen. Toggle it with O.

  • Green square = Paper Game rig ON  |  Red = OFF
  • Item icon = currently active side item
  • Labels: PG BJ 50 — shows which modules are active
📄
Paper Game

Rigs the Paper Game dispenser minigame. Set two item sides (A & B) — the mod manipulates the digit displayed on items inside dispensers so your active side always wins. Press H to switch sides, G to toggle mid-game.

🃏
Blackjack

Rigs Blackjack by renaming card items to display specific values. Enable Force 21 to make the first two cards show "A" and "10" — instant blackjack. Toggle with J / K.

🎲
50/50

Rigs 50/50 gambles by renaming win/lose items in your inventory. Three modes: Always Win, Always Lose, or Alternate. The server's name check sees whatever you set. Toggle with U.

🎯
Russian Roulette

Swaps your sword for a totem visually — you always look safe. Hold L to reveal the real sword in your hotbar slot. Release to swap back. Full control over when the "gun fires". Toggle with I.

Installation

  • Install Fabric Loader ≥ 0.16.0 and Fabric API for Minecraft 1.21
  • Copy DonutCasino.jar into your .minecraft/mods/ folder
  • Launch the game and press P to open settings
